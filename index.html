How to Play

  • Each cell contains a mini Tic Tac Toe board
  • Your move position determines where your opponent plays next
  • Win mini boards to claim them on the macro board
  • Get 3 macro board cells in a row to win!
  • If sent to a won/full board, you can play anywhere
